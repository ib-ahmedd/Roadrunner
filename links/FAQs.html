Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some questions that have been asked.

How can I Create a new account?

Register as customer: [ I want to order rides to deliver my packages]

  • Fill in your details
  • Create password
  • Verify email address/Account
  • Fill in your Bo-data
  • Get started

Register as a rider: [ I want to get requests to deliver packages]

  • Fill in your details
  • Create password
  • Verify account via email/sms
  • Fill in your Bo-data
  • Upload license and vehicle document
  • Get started

Register as a business: [ I am an individual/company with a fleet of riders]

  • Fill in your details/ company information
  • Create password
  • Verify email address/Account
  • Fill in your Bo-data
  • Fill in business data
  • Add riders and vehicle details
  • Get started
