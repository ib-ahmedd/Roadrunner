About Us

Road Runner is a global logistics and mobility fin-tech platform that handles all of your logistics and revenue-based vehicle financing. The company was birthed in 2022 to help bridge the gap between you and having your parcels delivered in no time. We're a team of dedicated, efficient and reliable team players, and we help deliver your package to your desired destination. Your convenience is our priority. Book a delivery today.
